How It Works

Download in
3 Easy Steps

  1. 1

    Copy the YouTube URL

    Open any YouTube video, Shorts, or playlist. Copy the URL from the address bar, or use YouTube's "Share" button to get the link.

  2. 2

    Paste & Fetch Formats

    Paste the URL into the YTSAVE input above and click "Get Links." We'll fetch the title, thumbnail, and all available video and audio formats instantly.

    Choose Quality & Download

    Pick your preferred resolution from the Video or Audio sections and tap Download. The file saves directly to your device — no middleman.
